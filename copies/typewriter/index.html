Swiss Cycle Messenger Championship

The aim of this project was, in addition to an appealing design concept, to do justice to the passion of bike couriers, to develop a communication strategy that generates viewers and motivates and appreciates the participants of the competition. As part of this final project for graphic designer EFZ, a market analysis and target group definition were carried out in two intensive weeks in advance and documented in a 24-page dossier ↓ . Based on this, a design concept with various media was designed. The result with the previous achievements was rewarded for the best graduation.

A pleasure brand in four weeks

This order includes a new appearance and packaging design from Salmundo: A salt trader who currently offers three rock salts in raw chunks for grating. The luxury brand tells its story, origin and use. The high-quality design language, color, font selection and storytelling positions the brand in a higher price segment.
